CSE Depatment

  • The Department of Computer Science & Engineering was established in the year 1989 along with the inception of college. The department is offering Under Graduate & Post Graduate programs in CSE & CST respectively. The intake for UG is 180 and for MTech CST are 18. From the academic year 2021-2022 B.Tech program in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science was introduced. Since the inception the department has regarded enormous growth over the time & met the challenges from the industry from the field of engineering & technical education. The management has provided all the necessary requirements for the department from time to time with the dynamic requirements of industry. The department has a teaching staff with PhD and has the distinction of having staff with MTech qualification only.
  • The department has modern laboratories to serve the teaching and research needs of the students of the department. The department has procured required number of licenses for software's like Microsoft Desktop Education all language license/software assurance packs and also other software's like ORACLE, Rational Rose. In addition to these software's the department is encouraging its staff & students to use the freeware software's. The department is well equipped with more than 300 computers with state of the art latest configuration. The students can experience a great facility of a total networked environment. The department is using 10no's of high-end servers for fulfilling the requirements of the students.
  • In central library of the college with a book capacity of about 45,000 books cater the needs of the students. In addition to the book collection, the central library provides the facility of e-books, print & e-journals, online lessons, video lessons for the benefit of students. In addition to the facilities provided by the central library the CSE department maintains a department library with a collection of 1500 books to substantiate the requirements of students. The department provides access to internet facility to its students with a bandwidth of 20mbps with which the students can enrich their professional skills.
  • The students of the department are exposed to co-curricular and extra-curricular activities at the college level, inter college level & university level. The student association CLASIC- CRRs Lustrous And Surmounting Intellectuals of Computer Science provides a platform to the student of the CSE department to exhibit their academic, cultural, sports & other talents. Majority of the students of the department are members of the professional society CSI- "Computer Society of India" with which the students are exposed to various events conducted by the CSI at country level. In addition to the regular academic training the students of the department will be given training for GATE, GRE, CAT, TOEFL & other competitive examinations. The students will be given CRT training for placements that visit our campus for placements. Majority of eligible students will be either absorbed by MNC's for placement or may opt for higher education in India or abroad.

Central Library

  • The Central Library is the significant feature of the college. It is constructed in the campus with a total built up area of 3,717 square meters at the cost of Rs 3 crores with all modern facilities. To the conventional library facilities with 286 seating capacity, digital library, discussion rooms, internet browsing centre and video conferencing hall with 120 seating capacity are added. At present the college library has about 52,548 volumes with 18,689 titles, and subscribes Springer Nature e- Journals Engineering Collection, Computer Science Collection & Business & Management Collection-- 123 Journals & 34 Magazines. It is being developed to meet the needs of the post graduate and doctoral candidates.
  • The library has over 4648 volumes and 4216 titles in the reference section that supports the activities of the research and development wing of the college. Digital Library with media server- 30 systems and Internet facility is available in the library with 70 systems. The college library is fully automated. The software is developed by the students of this college. The main features of the library software are Bar code based circulation system, web based public on line catalogue, Provision for serials control and stock verification etc.

Hostel

  • Boys Hostel is established in the year 2006 to provide accommodation and mess facilities to students of Sir C R Reddy College of Engineering. The hostel accommodates 800 students. Each room is attached with separate Bathroom. The rooms are furnished with Cots, Fans, Tables, Chairs, and Shelves with Iron Doors. Washbasin with mirror and Mosquito nets to window frames are also furnished. Internet facility & Hygienic drinking water is available to the inmates of the hostel.
  • Kitchen with modern cocking facilities and spacious dining hall is provided for inmates. Reading room, Indoor sport facilities are provided to the hostel inmates. A/c, Non A/c Guest rooms for College Guests/visitors and Parent's room for visiting parents are available in the hostel.
  • Co-operative store sports and medical facilities to the inmates are available in the college campus. A 24-hour security is available to the inmates of the hostel to protect the hostel property and to report incidents that require immediate attention to authorities.
  • Girls Hostel is established in the year 2002 to provide accommodation and mess facilities to students of Sir C R Reddy College of Engineering. The hostel accommodates about 500 students. Each room is attached with separate Bathroom.
